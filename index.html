What are we doing?

We are creating an iOS app to be used at our school's smoothie bar. This app will help students place orders right on the app, to save them time from having to order at the smoothie bar.

How can you can get involved?

1.) Well you can come up with creative names for the shakes, I will pick 2 lucky winners, to have their shake name on the app itself. 2.) You can create a cool logo for us 3.) Tell all your friends, to spread the word!
